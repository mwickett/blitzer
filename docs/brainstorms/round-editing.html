Round Editing — Interaction Flow

Three scenarios: undo right after submit, editing an older round via the history table, and what the table looks like when tappable

1 Just Submitted — Undo Toast

Immediately after tapping "Submit Round", a toast appears for ~5 seconds. One tap to undo. Disappears quietly — no interruption if the score was correct.

Alt No Undo — Confirmation Instead

If the undo toast feels distracting: skip it entirely. Instead, after tapping Submit, briefly flash a confirmation with the round deltas, then transition to the next round. Editing is always via the table.
